DERYA AKAY
derya akay is an artist born in turkey in 1988. they live, work, learn & garden on the unceded lands of the musqueam, squamish and tsleil-waututh nations since 2005. derya has been collaborating on gardening & gardening related food projects in their practice and has been working with vivienne, garden don’t care, mt pleasant neighbourhood house, and elisabeth community garden as a part of this project & with salem and cristian on this website. they enjoy dumpster diving, then cooking for large groups of people; learning to write, writing to learn; alley walks & weeds & daydreaming; flavour combinations and hand made objects. derya’s recent exhibitions include “meydan” at polygon gallery, north vancouver (2021); “contact traces'' at the wattis institute, san francisco (2021); and “the neighbour’s plate” with dana qaddah and amna elnour at unit 17, vancouver (2020).
